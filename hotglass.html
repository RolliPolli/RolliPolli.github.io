Hot Glass

Project Description

I'm natually a crafty person, and have dreamed of blowing glass nearly all my life. Luckily this semester I have taken the opportunity to learn from the Pittsburgh Glass Center. This half-semester class teaches us all the basic techniques for gathering, handeling, shaping, and blowing hot glass. Along the way I have gained a few small burns and a deep appreciation for glass artists.
