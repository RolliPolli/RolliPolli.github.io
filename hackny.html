FlockApp: hackny fall 2013

Project Description

I worked with a 6-person team in a 24 hour-sprint to build a working entry for hackNY, fall 2013. To start, we first brainstormed several potential ideas, honed on one, then ideated on it. Our idea was to create a location-based chat web app using FourSquare's API. We divided up the jobs needed to complete the task, and I set to work on designing the interactive flow of the app with a teammate. After I ironed out the flow, layout, and interactions through wireframes, I pitched in with a bit of coding. This was my first hackathon.
