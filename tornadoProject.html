Tornado Project

Project Description

I created a print book about tornadoes in Oklahoma, my home state. I think people who live outside of Tornado Ally have some misconceptions about tornadoes, this book serves to help remedy that.The book is meant for anyone who wants a brief, straightforward introduction to tornadoes and those who are coming to visit Oklahoma.

Throughout this project, I have performed research on Tornado Ally, tornado formation, state facts of where and when tornadoes strike, what to do in a tornado, what the worst storms have been, and other tornado marginalia.

To design the book, I started by sketching out all the information I wanted to include in thumbnails, then moved on to Illustrator and InDesign to create my book. Along the way, I iterated my designs based on feedback from my professor and peers. Although this was origninally just a class project, have decided to continue this project on my own, and hope to one day turn it into a published book.
