Sketching

Project Description

I believe that sketching is one of the most valuable skills for a UX designer. Through practicing my sketching, I am sharpening my ability to share my ideas quickly and visually.

In the process of sketching, I reflect and respond to what I am doing and thinking all at the same time. I then use these perceptions to continuously adjust my next actions. This is a process that I not only apply to sketching, but to my design work overall.

Subject Study: Cockatiel

Within this project, I focused on all the different parts of a cockatiel. I spent time studying the form and mechanics of the beak, wings, and feet before combining everything I learned into an understandable whole.
