The goal of this project was to depict an everyday process that you could use to teach to a kindergartener. Before starting on the last draft, I did quick sketches for each step. I then refined these sketches to make more sense. Here is a sample of a final sketch page:




Subject Study: Cockatiel

Within this project, I focused on all the different parts of a cockatiel. I spent time studying the form and mechanics of the beak, wings, and feet before combining everything I learned into an understandable whole.
