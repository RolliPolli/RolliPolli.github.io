Sketching

Project Description

I believe that sketching is one of the most valuable skills for a UX designer. With Sketching I am sharpening my ability to share my ideas quickly and visually.

To continue to improve in sketching, I am currently in a class where I am learning the fundementals (line, pressure, repetition, etc.). With the fundementals in mind, we then use those to build how to sketch in perspective and create meaningful life-like sketches.

In the process of sketching, I reflect and respond to what I am doing and thinking all at the same time. I then use these perceptions to continuously adjust my next actions. This is a process that I not only apply to sketching, but to design work overall.

The class runs to May 2014.
