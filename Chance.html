Chance App

Concept Mobile App

Chance is a mobile app that tracks your bike commute and matches you with other cyclists based on your path.

Techniques

  • Sketching
  • Wireframing
  • Interviewing
  • Personas

Technology

Duration

Left: a person's Chance matches.

Right: A person's match map with a fellow biker.


Project Goal

Work with a team to develop a useful concept for commuters.

Outcome

After guerilla research and interviewing commuters, we brainstormed all kinds of crazy ideas to help bikers, but then decided to focus on bike-commuters and strengthening the ephemeral relationships they make with other bikers on their bike rides.

We then developed an app that tracks your path as you ride, and matches you with other bikers whose paths cross yours, even if you didn't realize they do. You can then see your matches based on your path intersections, and message people who you'd like to reach out to, maybe for a bike ride together.

Process

  • Research potential ideas within the topic "commuting."
  • Interview bikers about their commutes and brainstorm ideas.
  • Sketch out workflow and wireframes for the app and speed date idea with bike commuters.
  • Craft a persona, Adriana, and scenarios of use for the app.
  • Create prototypes of the app demonstrating use in scenarios.
  • Pitch idea to professors and peers.


Sample Scenario:

  • Adriana opens the app, sees a list of potential dates, and is taps on Thomas’ picture.
  • She views the map that shows the areas (in yellow) where they both bike during their daily rides.
  • She views his full profile. He seems interesting, so she thinks she’ll message him.
  • She swipes upward to bring up the message window.
